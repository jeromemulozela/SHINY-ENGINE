Who I Am

I'm Zangi — a self-taught developer and designer from Zambia, driven by curiosity and a deep love for the web. I started coding by accident (aren't the best stories always accidental?) and got completely hooked by the ability to build something from nothing using just a keyboard.

How I Work

My approach centers on the details that matter: thoughtful spacing, smooth interactions, clean architecture. I believe the best digital experiences are ones where the design feels so natural that users never think about it — they just feel it. That invisible polish is what I chase in every project.

Beyond the Screen

When I'm not building, I'm pushing my limits through calisthenics, creating art, and building my content presence. I approach personal growth the same way I approach development: incrementally, intentionally, and relentlessly. I believe mastery in one discipline teaches you to master others.

The Goal

To build a career doing meaningful work — crafting digital products that solve real problems for real people. Every project is a step toward that, and every satisfied client is proof that the direction is right.

What Drives Me

Core Values

Details First

Every pixel matters. I obsess over spacing, typography, and micro-interactions so users feel quality even when they can't explain why.

Performance Always

Beautiful sites must also be fast. Every project is built with performance as a priority — lean code, optimized assets, real results.

Transparent Communication

No jargon, no guessing games. I keep clients informed throughout and make sure what's built is exactly what was envisioned.

Continuous Growth

The web never stops evolving and neither do I. I stay current with modern tools and emerging patterns to always bring fresh thinking.
